Too much Catnip

There was a ginger cat who

despite all odds, was serving as a gunner on a spaceship headed to mars

An alien is on the horizon, but the gunner knows what needs to be done

and so, he put dow his gun, for there was nothing he could say.

he then meowed so loudly the neighbour woke up!

the neighbour shot a bolt of superheated plasma at him, which was nimbly dodged

'darn, that was really close', the neighbour says, dissapointed at the missed shot.

until it hits a wall behind it causing it to topple over

he cries for his dog friend to help him up

but they pull out a gun, uttering the ironic opening line from fallout new vegas, pointing it at their 'friend'

'bazinga!' they say, gun in hand. The enemy is utterly confused - so much so that it is still and unmoving.

As confused as the enemy is, the narrator and everyone else is even more confused!
